Why is visualization effective?

According to Cairo, the human brain is effective at mentally visualizing numbers that are well rounded. We have an instictive idea about 72 versus 100. However, the human brain is not as effective at picturing other forms of numbers without aid. Visual aides help the human brain envision data in a way that cannot be done alone.
